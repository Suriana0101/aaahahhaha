About Lee Felix

Lee Felix is a 24 years old member of the South-Korean K-pop group "Stray Kids", formed by JYP Entertainment.Born on September 15, 2000, in Seven Hills, Sydney, Australia, he is of Korean descent. He started his career at the age of 17. Felix is the group's rapper and dancer, recognized for his deep voice and captivating charisma. He is fluent in both English and Korean,which is good for his career. One of his passions is cooking, particularly baking, with chocolate desserts being his favorite. On stage, Felix impresses with his energy and exceptional dance skills. His friendly personality and sincerity make him stand out both within the group and among fans. Felix inspires many with his hard work and positive outlook on life. His style and unique talents have made him one of the most iconic members of Stray Kids.
